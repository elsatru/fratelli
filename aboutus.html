Meet Mama and Sloth Fratelli

“A cooking vacation you’ll never forget”

With a lifetime of taking care and cooking for her family behind her, Agatha is happy to share all her acquired knowledge with the wider community.

Agatha loves cooking

She has put together a great selection of sweet and savoury Italian cooking courses for young and old, newbies or experienced cooks.

She's been cooking for 40 years

She’s also opened up her home for people to come and stay and enjoy the amazing nature that surrounds the retreat.

Sloth loves pastries and deserts and after years of being tied up in the kitchen he has turned into the best sous-chef Mama Fratelli could hope for.

Sloth is fun camp leader

Responsible for the Dolce cooking course, his bubbly personality makes him the best teacher for the children’s cooking course and your kids will never forget him.

He's written a cookery book for kids

As camp leader, he’s also put together a great variety of outdoor activities to fill your time in between classes.
